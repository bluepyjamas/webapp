A letter and a melody for

Hannah del Pilar

For You

Chapter I: The Little Things

It started with the quiet, little things—the moments you probably didn't even notice. When we first became friends, I found myself slowly captivated by the beautiful details of who you are. I have always appreciated your incredible taste in music; the songs you share always seem to carry a certain kind of magic. I admire the fiercely beautiful way you love and care for your family. But more than anything, seeing the depth of your faith, the way you love God, and how you selflessly make time to uplift the people around you... that was when admiration turned into something much deeper. That was what made me truly start to fall for you.

Chapter II: The Realization

I started to care for you as a friend first, but somewhere along the way, the ground beneath me shifted without me even realizing it. I remember looking at you one day, and a very clear, sudden thought crossed my mind: "I really want to spend my whole life with you." It wasn't just a fleeting thought; it was a profound realization of how much space you have taken up in my heart. I realized just how incredibly much you mean to me, and the simple truth is, I do not ever want to face a future where you aren't in my life.

Chapter III: My Hope

I built this just for you, as a way to lay my cards on the table and let you know exactly where my heart is. I want you to know that I am always here in your corner, and I am always waiting. I am holding onto the quiet, persistent hope that someday—if you are open to it, and if there is even the smallest chance—there could be a real "us" in the future. I don't want to rush you, I just needed you to know that you are deeply loved and cherished.

Chapter IV: The Future

Whatever happens next, please know that my intentions are pure. I value our connection above all else, and I will always protect it. But if you ever find yourself looking at me the way I have learned to look at you, know that my heart is ready. Until then, I will keep cheering you on, supporting your dreams, and appreciating every single moment I get to spend with you. Thank you for simply being you, Han.
